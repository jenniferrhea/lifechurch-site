The Chazown Experience

Discover and pursue God's dream for your life.

Each of us is a masterpiece, placed on earth for a unique purpose that's solely ours to fulfill.

Through this interactive campus event, you will examine your core values, spiritual gifts, and past experiences. These three areas will help you identify common themes, bring clarity to your unique passions, and lead you to the purpose God has for just for you. The best part is that you can start planning your next steps toward pursuing God's vision–or chazown–for your life.

Meet the Faces of Chazown

The Chazown Experience is taught by Pastor Craig Groeschel based on his book, Chazown. He will give you the biblical foundation and practical teaching to God's dreams for you.

Throughout the Chazown Experience, five people who are living out their chazowns will share their experiences with you on video and offer tips to help you find your next steps.

“I’ve been through some impossible situations. But through all of them, I’ve seen God shape me into a leader. Now my chazown is to help others navigate their own tough situations and show them God’s love on the other side.”

Finding Your Chazown

Get ready for a fun, powerful, and challenging experience! During this two-day campus event, you will receive a participant guide to use as Pastor Craig guides you through each step of identifying your chazown. The guide includes everything you'll need to follow along, take notes, and create a plan for your future.

“I’ve lived through a miracle—God delivered my husband and me from the bondage of addiction! My chazown is to be a light to others lost in the darkness of addiction.”

Rae Anne   tweet this quote

Track Your Progress

What good is a plan without taking the next step? An important part of the Chazown Experience is tracking your progress in the future. The participant guide includes specific features that you can use after the event as a reminder and a motivator to live out your chazown each day.
