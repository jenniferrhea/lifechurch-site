Mobile Smart Giving

You can give tithes and offerings to LifeChurch.tv via SMS.

You can give tithes and offerings to LifeChurch.tv via SMS.

Tithing

SMS GIVE and the amount of your gift to 86613.
For example, GIVE $100.

Offerings (learn more)

SMS SPACES, RELIEF, or DIGITAL followed by the amount to 86613 to make your designated gift.
For example, SPACES $75.

Account Updates

Need to update your account? SMS EDIT to 86613.

Non-Cash Giving

Did you know you can donate non-cash gifts like stocks, bonds, mutual funds, or cars?

Did you know you can donate non-cash gifts like stocks, bonds, mutual funds, or cars? There are many other ways to give:

  • Marketable Securities: transfer ownership of your stocks, bonds, or mutual fund shares
  • Other Non-Cash Gifts: donate items like cars, boats, and jewelry
  • Life Insurance: name LifeChurch.tv as a beneficiary of your policy
  • Planned Gifts: give via retirement assets, bequests, trusts, etc. as part of your overall personal financial strategy.

If you are interested in any of these types of giving, please contact us at giving@lifechurch.tv.
